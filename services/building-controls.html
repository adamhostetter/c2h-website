Building Controls

Design, installation, and integration of building automation systems — from a single rooftop unit's BACnet feed up to a campus-wide controls overhaul. Open protocols, no proprietary lock-in.

Capabilities

Controls work that pays for itself.

A well-tuned controls system cuts energy spend 10-20% on day one. We design, install, and tune systems that keep delivering — not ones that drift back to defaults six months later.

BAS design & installation

New construction and retrofit — sequence of operations, panel layout, point lists, field wiring, head-end programming, and commissioning.

Multi-vendor integration

Tridium Niagara, JCI Metasys, Siemens Desigo, Honeywell, Distech, Automated Logic — we speak every dialect, no vendor lock-in.

Open protocols

BACnet IP/MSTP, Modbus TCP/RTU, LonWorks, MQTT — interoperable systems you can extend or replace without ripping it all out.

Energy & analytics

Trend reviews, fault detection diagnostics, energy benchmarking — find the buildings that are bleeding spend and fix them first.

Cybersecurity hardening

Network segmentation, role-based access, password rotation, firmware patching — controls systems that won't end up on a CISA advisory.

Recommissioning

Existing systems audited, sequences re-tuned, drifted setpoints corrected — recover the savings the original design promised.
